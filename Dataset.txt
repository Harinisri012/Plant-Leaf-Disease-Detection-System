# Plant Leaf Disease Detection Dataset

The dataset used for the Plant Leaf Disease Detection System can be found at the following link:

[Plant Disease Recognition Dataset on Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rashikrahmanpritom/plant-disease-recognition-dataset)

This dataset contains images of plant leaves categorized into different types of diseases, including healthy leaves and various disease categories. It is used to train and evaluate the machine learning models for detecting plant diseases.
